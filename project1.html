Summary of Project's Key Features and Benefits

Our AI-powered fitness app is designed to provide personalized workout plans and nutrition advice tailored to each user’s unique goals. By utilizing data scraped from public sources like Kaggle, our app delivers customized workout routines based on the user’s fitnes goals, body type, fitness level, and dietary preferences. We also implemented a chatbot that enhances the user experience by answering questions and guiding users through their fitness journey. Whether users want to build muscle, or follow specific diet plans, the app adapts to their individual needs.

Overview of how Project Works

The app collects user inputs, such as their fitness goals, body type, and dietary restrictions, and uses this data to generate personalized workout and meal plans. The AI scrapes fitness and nutrition data from public databases, ensuring up-to-date recommendations. The chatbot interacts with users to refine their workout preferences, suggest exercises, or address other questions in real-time. Future planned updates will expand the app's capabilities to include diet recommendations tailored to specific allergies and schedule management to fit workout sessions appropriately within the users daily / weekly routine. This will make our app a comprehensive tool for both fitness and nutrition guidance, prioritizing personalized user-experience.

Technologies Used

  • Frontend: HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, React, Angular, Flutter, Figma
  • Backend: Node.js, Express.js, SQL, NoSQL
  • Tooling: npm, Git, CI/CD Pipelines
